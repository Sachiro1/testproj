Enchanted River
The Enchanted River is famous for its deep blue crystal-clear water. Many tourists visit to swim and watch the fish feeding.
Siargao Island
Siargao Island is the most famous tourist destination in Mindanao. It is known as the Surfing Capital of the Philippines because of the famous Cloud 9 surfing spot. Tourists also enjoy island hopping and lagoons.
Tinuy-an Falls
Tinuy-an Falls is one of the widest waterfalls in the Philippines. It is sometimes called the “Little Niagara Falls of the Philippines.”
Mount Apo
KMount Apo is the highest mountain in the Philippines. It attracts hikers and adventure travelers who want to experience nature and wildlife.
Samal Island
Samal Island is a popular beach destination near Davao City. It is known for white sand beaches, waterfalls, and island resorts.
Maria Cristina Falls
Maria Cristina Falls is one of the most famous waterfalls in Mindanao. It is known as the “Twin Falls” and helps generate hydroelectric power.
