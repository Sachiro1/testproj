"THE PHILIPPINES"

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian country made up of more than 7,600
island in the western Pacific Ocean.Its capital city is Manila, and it is known for its rich
culture,friendly people, and beautiful natural landscapes.The country has a mix of
Asian and Western influences due to its history with Spain and the United States.
The official languages are Filipino and English. Tourism is very important in
the Philippines because of its stunning beaches, mountains, and historical landmarks.

Mayon Volcano One of the best tourist spots in Luzon is Mayon Volcano, located
in Albay province. It is famous for its perfect cone shape, making it one of the most
beautiful volcanoes in the world. Tourists visit to take pictures, explore the nearby
Cagsawa Ruins, and enjoy activities like ATV rides around the lava trails.

The best tourist spot in the Visayas is Boracay. It is famous for its powdery white sand,
clear blue water, and beautiful sunsets. Located in Aklan, Boracay is perfect for
swimming, island hopping, and water activities like parasailing and snorkeling
.Many tourists visit this island because of its relaxing and lively atmosphere.

The best tourist spot in Mindanao is Siargao. It is known as the “Surfing Capital of the Philippines.” Tourists visit Cloud 9, one of the most popular surfing areas in the country. Aside from surfing, visitors enjoy lagoons, rock pools, and peaceful island views.
