Chocolate Hills
TThe Chocolate Hills are a renowned geological formation in Bohol, Philippines, consisting of 1,260 to over 1,770 symmetrical, cone-shaped limestone mounds spanning over 50 square kilometers. Ranging from 30 to 120 meters in height, these hills are covered in grass that turns brown during the dry season, creating a chocolate-like appearance.
Boracay
Boracay is a small, world-renowned resort island in the Philippines (Aklan province), famed for its powdery white sand beaches specifically the 4-km White Beach crystal clear turquoise waters, and vibrant nightlife. Located south of Manila, it offers a blend of relaxation, bustling beach culture, and water sports like scuba diving and windsurfing.
Bantayan Island
Bantayan Island is a tranquil, laid-back paradise in northern Cebu, Philippines, renowned for its crystal-clear turquoise waters and fine white-sand beaches, particularly in Santa Fe.
Kalanggaman Island
Kalanggaman Island, located in Palompon, Leyte, is a stunning, uninhabited paradise famous for its long, powdery white sandbars, crystal clear turquoise waters, and serene atmosphere. Often cited as one of the Philippines' most beautiful, this eco tourism spot features no luxury hotels only raw nature, making it ideal for snorkeling, swimming, and camping.
Oslob Whale Shark Watching
Oslob Whale Shark Watching in Cebu, Philippines, offers a popular, highly regulated, and early-morning activity to see and swim with massive whale sharks (butanding) in their natural habitat.
Kawasan Falls
Kawasan Falls is a famous, three-stage,, multi-tiered waterfall system in Badian, Cebu, Philippines, renowned for its stunning, crystal-clear turquoise water. Nestled in a lush jungle at the foot of the Mantalongon Mountain Range, it is a premier destination for swimming, relaxing, and thrilling,, canyoneering activities.
