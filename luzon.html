Mayon Volcano
Mayon Volcano, located in Albay, Philippines, is an active stratovolcano renowned for its near-perfect conical shape. Rising 2,462 meters (8,077 ft), it is the Philippines' most active volcano, with over 50 recorded eruptions since 1616, often causing significant evacuations. It is a major tourist destination and a protected natural park
Baguio City
Baguio is the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" and "City of Pines," a high-altitude city in Northern Luzon known for its cool climate, pine trees, and vibrant, artistic, and indigenous culture.
Puero Princesa
Puerto Princesa, the capital of Palawan, is a top Philippine eco-tourism destination known as the "City in a Forest". As the country's largest city, it acts as a gateway to natural wonders, most notably the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River, alongside snorkeling at Honda Bay.
Batanes
Batanes is the Philippines' smallest and northernmost province, renowned as the "Home of the Winds." This scenic archipelago offers dramatic, rolling hills reminiscent of New Zealand, iconic stone houses, and a slow-paced, tranquil lifestyle. It is a unique destination featuring rich Ivatan culture, rugged cliffs, and pristine, windy landscapes.
